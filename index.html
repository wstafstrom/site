About Me

Hi, I'm Will, a PhD Candidate in Dr. Rebecca Nelson's lab at Cornell University. I work on integrating diverse approaches for mitigating maize mycotoxins. My research spans scales from the genetic (mapping maize resistance and yield-component loci), to the organismal (detecting mycotoxins via physical and spectral methods), to the landscape (using large geospatial datasets to predict mycotoxin hotspots).
